About Us

Helping Mauritians in Australia build and maintain their dream properties in Mauritius.

Are you a Mauritian living in Australia, looking to build, renovate, or manage property back home? Managing a real estate project remotely can be challenging—coordinating builders, ensuring quality, and making payments securely. That’s where Dodo Property Services comes in!

We are your trusted partner in real estate project management, construction, and property maintenance in Mauritius, giving you complete peace of mind while you remain in Australia.

Why Work With Us?

  • Hassle-Free Project Management: We bridge the gap between you and builders in Mauritius. Our team handles communication, negotiations, and project updates, so you stay informed without the stress.
  • Independent Quality Assurance: Our on-ground inspectors ensure all work meets specifications before releasing payments. We provide detailed reports, photos, and progress updates, so you get full transparency.
  • Secure Payment Management: Funds are held safely until milestones are completed to your satisfaction. This protects both you and the builder, ensuring work is completed before payment.
  • Full Suite of Property Services: Whether you’re building from scratch, renovating, or maintaining your property, we have you covered.
